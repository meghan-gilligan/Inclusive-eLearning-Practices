Introduce Yourself

Author: Mary grace Dela fuente

Posted: 10/28/2025, 8:22:56 PM

This quick activity allows us to build a small community of practice focused on inclusive design.

Please introduce yourself to the group by sharing the following three items:

  1. Your Name and Role: What do you do (e.g., college professor, instructional designer, education student, HR trainer)?

  2. Your "Why": Why are you interested in inclusive or accessible eLearning practices? (Keep it brief.)

  3. Your Go-To Strategy: Share one specific design feature or organizational strategy in an online course that has personally helped you stay organized and focused as a learner or teacher.

After you post, we encourage you to read someone else's and offer a quick word of welcome to a colleague!
