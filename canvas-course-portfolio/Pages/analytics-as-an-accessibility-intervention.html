Analytics as an Accessibility Intervention

The complexity of having instructors use multiple platforms in one course creates a highly fractured and confusing environment (Cabral et al., 2025). For neurodivergent students, this results in cognitive overload, making it hard to navigate numerous systems and focus on instructional content (Cabral et al., 2025; Le Cunff et al., 2024).

This reading explains how LMS analytics are not just for grading; they are a necessary accessibility tool that helps mitigate these barriers.
