📝 Example 2: Assignment - Climate Change Research
❌ BEFORE: Single Path, Rigid Format
Week 5 Assignment: Climate Change Essay
Assignment Instructions

Write a 1500-word essay analyzing the impacts of climate change on global ecosystems.

Requirements:

  • Must be in 5-paragraph essay format
  • Times New Roman, 12pt font, double-spaced
  • Submit as a Word document (.docx only)
  • Minimum 5 academic sources
⏰ Due: Friday, 11:59 PM (no extensions)

Grading: Essays that do not follow the 5-paragraph format will receive a maximum grade of C.

Resources

Read Chapter 8 in your textbook (PDF posted in Week 5 module)

❌ The Problems: Only ONE format accepted • No choice in expression • Talented students who struggle with traditional essays are excluded • Single resource type • Rigid deadline
✅ AFTER: Multiple Paths, Flexible Expression
Week 5 Project: Climate Change Impact Analysis
Learning Objectives

By completing this project, you will demonstrate your ability to:

  • Analyze the scientific evidence for climate change impacts
  • Evaluate effects on global ecosystems
  • Synthesize information from multiple credible sources
  • Communicate complex scientific concepts effectively

✅ CHOOSE YOUR FORMAT: Select the format that best showcases your strengths. All options are assessed using the same rubric and learning objectives.

📄 Option A: Written Research Paper (1500 words)
🎨 Option B: Visual Infographic (with 500-word analysis)
🎙️ Option C: Podcast Episode (10-12 minutes + transcript)
📊 Option D: Slide Presentation (12-15 slides + speaker notes)

All formats must include minimum 5 credible sources and demonstrate mastery of learning objectives.

📚 Resources (Choose What Works for You)
📖 Chapter 8 Reading (PDF)
🎧 Chapter 8 Audio Version
🎥 Video Lecture with Captions
📊 Visual Data Summary
⏰ Flexible Deadline Options:
  • Standard: Friday, 11:59 PM
  • Extended: Sunday, 11:59 PM (no penalty - just let me know by Wednesday)
✅ The Solutions: Multiple expression formats • Same high standards, different paths • Resources in multiple formats • Flexible deadlines • Students showcase their strengths
